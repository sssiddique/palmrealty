MEET THE FOUNDER

KERRY KLUN

Kerry has been a full time real estate broker for over 30 years. She is an expert at representing buyers and sellers, and has the experience necessary to assist you in navigating today's complex real estate market. Kerry is the Managing Broker/Owner of Palm Realty. Palm Realty operates on a paperless transacation basis reducing our carbon footprint. Kerry's goal is to give you the finest real estate buying or selling experience available anywhere. Before, during, and after the sale you can count on Kerry to be available to answer your questions in an honest and matter of fact way. Give her a call so she can set up an area tour and show you the schools, shopping, medical facilities, and of course the great amenities the Space Coast has to offer! Ask Kerry to share with you what she loves most about living the beachside lifestyle.

Kerry lives beachside with her rescued fur babies! In her spare time, Kerry enjoys cooking, animal rescue, anything to do with the ocean - specifically boating and SCUBA diving, interior design, the arts and much more!

Kerry would love to represent you with your Florida home needs!!!
